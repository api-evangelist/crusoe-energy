Crusoe Cloud earned NVIDIA Exemplar Cloud validation for large-scale AI training on NVIDIA HGX B200 systems, running 8 frontier models across multiple precisions on 512 NVIDIA GPUs powered entirely by renewable energy. The validation tested real-world training workloads across multiple model families and precision formats, confirming predictable, efficient performance for customers training AI models on Crusoe's infrastructure.
